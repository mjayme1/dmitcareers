Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Specialist

Overview

A search engine optimization specialist (or SEO specialist) is someone who analyzes a client's website and implements any necessary changes in order for it to be optimized for search engines. Search engine optimization specialists can be a company employee, an individual consultant, or can work for an SEO consulting firm. These specialists understand how to write specific SEO copy and have in-depth knowledge when it comes to keyword search. If a company (big or small) relies on web traffic for their sales or marketing, hiring a search engine optimization specialist is key to having a successful and profitable business.

Salary

$49,521 per year

The average salary for a SEO specialist is $49,521 per year in Canada.

Skills Required: Technical Skills

Being a great SEO specialist requires a wide range of skills. The skills listed are necessary for an effective SEO manager, but being familiar with technical aspects, search engine algorithms, keyword research, and link building is especially important, as is the ability to work with tools that improve SEO performance and make an SEO expert's work routine easier. Some of the most important technical skills for an SEO specialist are:

  1. 1. Google Tools

    Knowledge of Google tools like Google Analytics and Google Search Console gives an SEO specialist an edge, and are crucial today for any professional who wants to stay on top of the latest SEO trends and track the outcomes and effectiveness of campaigns and operations.

  2. 2. HTML + CSS + JavaScript

    Basic coding knowledge is almost obligatory, so they cannot be overlooked. You should be able to look at a website's source code and determine the essential elements that are relevant to your campaign. You should also be able to make adjustments and swaps quickly and efficiently without disrupting your site.

  3. 3. Keyword Research

    Among other important SEO skills are keyword research, which is a robust SEO strategy when relevant and well done. The power of keyword research lies in better understanding your target market and how they are searching for your content, services, or products.

  4. 4. Link Building
  5. 5. Technical SEO
  6. 6. A/B Testing

Skills Required: Soft Skills

Below are some of the most important soft skills an SEO specialist should have:

  1. 1. Critical Thinking

    The first and most crucial skill on our list is analysis, which is a broad term that covers a wide range of SEO aspects. As an SEO professional, you must be able to take data about who, what, when, and where, and grasp the "how" and "why" questions that come with it.

  2. 2. Speaking & Writing Ability

    SEO requires not only confidence but the ability to simplify complex ideas and thoughts into concepts that non-SEOs can comprehend and use to make judgments.

  3. 3. Social Skills and Communication

    The value of communication in SEO canâ€™t be understated. Youâ€™ll need to communicate proactively with your other team members and/or clients to ensure your directives are carried out.

  4. 4. Fast Adaptability

    SEO also requires adaptability and thick skin. There's always something new to learn, and SEO tactics may constantly be improved. It's also crucial to be a fast learner because you'll be using a variety of platforms and tools to get the job done.
